Engineer / Producer / Mixer / Musician

Alan Weatherhead

Alan Weatherhead is a versatile producer, engineer, mixer, and multi-instrumentalist based in Asheville, NC. Meticulous in the studio and conversant in a broad range of musical styles, he has received praise for both his intricate orchestrations and his ability to capture a song's essence in simple, stripped-down settings. He has worked with Sparklehorse, Magnolia Electric Co., Mary Timony, Cracker/Camper Van Beethoven, Daniel Johnston, Lucero, LP, Darren Jessee and many more.
